Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2026

Information We Collect

The 2M Whites Pharma app collects the following information to provide its services:

  • Account information: name, phone number, email address, profile photo, and job role.
  • Field activity data: visit records, customer interactions, sales orders, stock transfers, and daily reports.
  • Customer data: names, contact details, addresses, and financial transaction history.
  • Device information: device identifiers, operating system version, and app usage logs for performance monitoring.

Location Data

This app requests access to your device's precise location (GPS). Location data is collected only when you perform a GPS check-in during a customer visit. It is used solely to verify and record the geographic coordinates of field visits for operational and reporting purposes. Location is never collected in the background or without your explicit action.

Contacts Access

This app requests access to your device contacts to allow you to quickly select a phone number when adding or updating customer information. Contact data is used only within the app for this purpose and is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Push Notifications

This app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications. Notifications are used to inform you about plan approvals, visit updates, and other operational alerts relevant to your role. You can manage notification preferences in your device settings.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) to protect data in transit. Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only. While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Firebase (Google): for authentication, push notifications, and crash reporting. Firebase's privacy policy applies to data processed by their services.
  • Google Play Services: for app updates and in-app review functionality.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Field activity data (visits, orders, reports) is retained for business reporting and audit purposes. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting the system administrator.

Your Rights

As a user of this application, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Object to processing of your personal information.

To exercise these rights, please contact the administrator.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of June 11, 2026 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will be in effect immediately after being posted on this page. We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time and you should check this Privacy Policy periodically.
